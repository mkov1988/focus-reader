## 1. Discovery Phase (The "Entrance")
*   **User Goal**: Find something interesting to read within the 60,000+ Gutenberg catalog.
*   **Touchpoints**: Search Bar, Keyword Icons, "Popular" and "Latest" horizontal shelves.
*   **Feeling**: Minimalist, organized, and vast.

## 2. Engagement Phase (The "Inspiration")
*   **User Goal**: Commit to a book and verify it meets their interest.
*   **Touchpoints**: Book Detail view, author metadata, "Quick Preview" snippet.
*   **Feeling**: Curiosity and preparation for focus.

## 3. Consumption Phase (The "Flow State")
*   **User Goal**: Absorb text with zero friction and maximum cognitive efficiency.
*   **Touchpoints**: Red Focal Letter, Sentence View centering, WPM sliders.
*   **Interaction**: Double-tap to Pause, Swipe to Scrub.
*   **Feeling**: Deep focus, rhythmic immersion, high velocity.

## 4. Retention Phase (The "Growth")
*   **User Goal**: Track their reading journey and keep what they learned.
*   **Touchpoints**: Persistent "Continue Reading" shelf, saved annotations, reading statistics.
*   **Feeling**: Accomplishment and continuous improvement.
